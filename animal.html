Animals

Here is a list of animals with some interesting facts:

  • Lions: Known as the king of the jungle, lions are social animals that live in groups called prides.
  • Elephants: The largest land animals on Earth, elephants have remarkable memories and are known for their strong social bonds.
  • Penguins: Flightless birds that are well adapted to life in the water, penguins primarily feed on fish and krill.
